Who

Who are we???

We believe that quality education is not a privilege but a fundamental right, and Pratitih brings these words to life.

4th among the 17 sustainable development goals of the UN, quality education. That's our sole objective, to be able to provide quality education for all by:

  • Hosting Student exchange programs
  • Prodiving resources and opportunities to learn round the clock
  • Finally, not missing out on any possible effort

Undoubtedly there is a large chunk of India's population that falls into the pool of literates, yet we consider quality eductaion a privilege and not a fundamental right?
